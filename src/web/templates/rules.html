These are the Rules you should abide to!!📏

  1. Starting the Game

    • Either select Single Player Game or the Multi Player Game
    • You can select them hovering the curser on to the single player space man or multi-player space man and clicking on him

  2. Single Player Rules

    • Wait until the instructions of the Game are displayed on the screen and just enjoy the music!!!
    • The Enemies starts coming up one after the other in packs
    • Shoot them to death until they dissappear or you will be dying. Use SPACEBAR as your shooting gun
    • You can move with standard UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT keys and kill all the enemies including main villain until one of them gives up
